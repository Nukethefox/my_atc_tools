Welcome to ATC Tools!

This is a series of tools to assist virtual air traffic controllers on decision-making related tasks.

This website has been designed for landscape desktop/tablet screens.

Main features:

  • Speed, distance and altitude units conversion

  • Transition Level calculator according to QNH and Transition Altitude

  • Active runway suggestion according to wind

  • Suggestion of departure prefference for multiple traffic, according to wake and speed category, SID and EOBT

  • Distance and ETA calculator for single traffic, according to its speed

  • Conflicts calculator for two traffic flying the same route, according to their speeds

  • Traffic separation calculator

Recommended steps before connecting:

(With list of useful links for IVAO Spain users)


  1. Make sure your headphones and microphone are working properly.

  2. Make sure your radar software is set up as you like it: insets/screens, labels, menus, center point and range, colors, sounds, COM & VHF...

  3. CheckNOTAMSfor your area.

  4. CheckWebeye foradjacent ATC stations and traffic status on your area.

  5. Check METAR and TAF, and determine andActive Runways and Transition Level.

  6. (METAR & TAF Text Decoder)

  7. (METAR & TAF Visual Decoder)

  8. Make sure yourRadio Cheat SheetandTraffic Separation Calculatoror Taxi Manager are ready.

  9. Get ready any documentation that could be useful during the ATC session:

  10. Airways Checker ("Buscador de aerovias")

  11. Airport Procedures & Cheat Sheet ("fichas de aerodromo")

  12. Connect to the Division's Discord Server for coordination with adjacent ATC.

  13. Before connecting, prepare the Remarks for the ATIS (Autoswitch,Datalink, Non Standard Procedures, Severe Weather, TWY in use tovacate RWY, Approach in use, HIRO, etc...)

  14. ONCE CONNECTED TO IVAO...

  15. AIRPORTS Window: Select controlled airports and activerunways.

  16. ATIS Window: Check ATIS Active, position callsign, active runways, TA & TL, copy and paste previously prepared remarks.

  17. VHF Window: Check frequency, adjust volume.

  18. COM Window: Check VHF1 frequency. Send "ONLINE" message on ATC tab.

  19. ATC Window: Open Direct Message for adjacent ATC.

  20. Remember to save profile.
